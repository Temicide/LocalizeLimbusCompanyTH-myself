## Ravi

### Appearance
Ravi is a man with short black hair and a tanner skintone. He wears black-rimmed square glasses, and a long maroon jacket with red and yellow stripes on the collar and sleeves. Beneath his jacket he wears a matching pair of trousers, a beige shirt, and a black tie. Like his fellow LCE employees, he wears an LCE armband on his left arm.

### Personality
Ravi is a mellow and rational member of the LCE Clerical Team who remains calm under the circumstances of the invasion. He is sociable enough to chat with his supposed colleague Sora, but also reclusive enough to stop talking to her when he grew tired of the topic. He expresses that is savvy enough to not say any clichés that usually result in character death, yet is ultimately an overly naïve and trusting new hire who was unable to sniff out the threat right in front of him.

### Story
Ravi's first day at the company was the day the House of Spiders' members attacked. A portion of the ceiling would fall on him during the chaos, trapping him beneath the rubble with Sora, who had disguised herself as an LCE employee. During their time trapped, Sora discussed with Ravi about what foods they wanted to eat after they made it out alive, to which Ravi eventually played dead in order to avoid talking about. Later, Dante, and the few Sinners they were with walked by, allowing Gregor to realize that survivors were in the hallway. Because Sora knew where the elevator was, she and Ravi were given the chance to join the faction of Sinners temporarily. After the group ran into Shiomi Yoru, however, Sora was reminded of her true allegiance to the House of Spiders and attempted to murder Dante for their cause. While the Sinners managed to protect their manager, Ravi was torn in half and killed during this attack.
