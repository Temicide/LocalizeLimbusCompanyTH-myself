## Tieqiu Boss

### Appearance
Tieqiu Boss has a full body prosthetic replacement, notably a very old model which hardly anyone uses anymore, which makes him stand out more even in The City where such procedures aren't unheard of. His body is made of a silver metal with a large, pale olive, dragon symbol across the front of it, sporting a pale eye in the center. His right arm is a large hammer, while his left is a claw.

### Personality
The boss of the Tieqiu Crew is quick to notice suspicious behavior and will confront people head on when he believes he is being cheated. He dislikes arrogant and self important people, as seen by his comments towards Sonya, and has little patience for other people in general.

### Story
The Tieqiu Boss is the leader of the Tieqiu Crew, a Syndicate of highly uncouth bruisers, and a participant in the game for the Golden Bough underneath a J Corp. casino. The game played out normally until its final round, when Rodion made a suspicious move that the Tieqiu Boss believes to be evidence of her using Wishpower. He called her out on this and demanded her to show her arm, which she agreed to. Upon pulling up her sleeve, he finds that no Wishpower had been used, much to his surprise and outrage. As punishment for falsely accusing another player of cheating, a trash compactor quickly crushed him to death.
