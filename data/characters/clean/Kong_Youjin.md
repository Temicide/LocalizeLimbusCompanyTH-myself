## Kong Youjin

### Appearance
Kong Youjin is a tall man with long, brown hair pulled back into a long ponytail fastened by a silver xiao guan, with notably curly facial hair, such as eyebrows and a moustache which curl at their ends, as well as a long goatee. He wears a long pale blue robe overtop a darker robe, the pale blue robe having a floral trim along its inner lining and sleeves. On his feet, he wears similarly blue loafers.

### Personality
Kong Youjin showed himself to be highly intelligent, heading a team of incredibly capable scientists among the Kong Family. Just as intelligent as he was, he cared greatly for his wife and unborn child, hoping for a great future for the people of Hongyuan.

### Story
**Prior to the events of Limbus Company***

Kong Youjin once met with Jia Mu, who established a plan to create a thirteenth Heishou Pack Branch, invented by a Bolus created by the Hierarch accompanied by the team of Kong Family scientists. In reality, this was a front, planned by Jia Mu to have the Kong Family annihilated. He would be thankful for a specialized Bolus given to his wife as supposedly a means to ensure good tidings for their unborn child.
At the day of the presentation, Kong Youjin and Sihui would sit front row for the presentation, yet would ultimately end up brutally killed by the combined efforts of the Niaojia-ren, Outskirts monsters propagated by Jia Mu's special Bolus given to Kong Sihui, and the Head arriving to kill the monsters.
Their history would be twisted into a sign of greed among the royal houses of Hongyuan, painting a false history of hubris.

**Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing**

The history of the Kong Family would be made known to the Sinners and Lin Daiyu, as Hong Lu experiences memories reemerging via the resonance of a nearby Golden Bough.
