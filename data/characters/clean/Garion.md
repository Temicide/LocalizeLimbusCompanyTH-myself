## Garion

### Appearance
Garion's appearance is near identical to her second form as Binah of L Corp. She possesses pale skin with pitch black pupils, a single mole positioned beneath her left eye. She has short, asymmetrical black hair which is largely shaved down on her left side, but falls to her shoulder along the right side of her face, where it is embellished with golden highlights. She wears an Arbiter's uniform, a black dress with a long, feathered coat laid atop it. She also wears boots. Garion's outfit is adorned with golden highlights, including in hexagonal patterns along the right side of her dress and the upper left side of her coat.

### Personality
Garion is a powerful and sadistic individual committed to her violent duties as an Arbiter of the Head. She describes herself as someone who found a thrill in death and destruction where nothing else had ever managed to impress her, and therefore enacts brutality with both delight and satisfaction. Her ruthlessness is not usually the forefront of her personality, however, and the other half of her disposition takes the form of a wise and patient woman with a strong understanding of the City's cruelties. Outside of her calls to action as a seasoned fighter, Garion also enjoys observing others, taking a relatively non-judgemental approach to understanding their reactions and mindsets.
The greater extent of her personality is seen through her reincarnation as Binah, who also maintains the sadism and sagaciousness which defines Garion. As a Sephirah of Lobotomy Corporation, Binah is shown to hold a heavy grudge against the man who tied her to such a fate, and a serious responsibility even towards duties she is not particularly fond of. She also cements herself as an extremely knowledgeable, perceptive person. After multiple lives leading into her position as the Patron Librarian of the Floor of Philosophy, Binah eventually finds herself a slightly more amiable woman with an interest in philosophical discussion and the well-being of those around her, expressed in her own, mysterious way.

### Trivia
Prior to the May 29, 2025 update, only the English and Japanese translations of Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing listed her name as "Garion". Following the update, both translations now match the original Korean of *Limbus Company*, where she is labelled "???" in the nameplate section of the dialogue UI.
This means that, within *Limbus Company*, Garion's name is only present in the credits of Canto VIII.
The name Binah** (Hebrew: בִּינָה; Bīnā) means "Understanding", and corresponds to the third sephira of the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, directly under Keter.
Her associated Sapling of Light power is "Superbia", which allows one to utilize any equipped E.G.O without using Sin resources.
