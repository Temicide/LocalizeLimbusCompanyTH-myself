## Alessio

### Appearance
Alessio is a tired looking man with shaggy black hair and half-lidded eyes. He wears the standard P Corp. Archival Department uniform of a skin-tight suit with orange patterning all the way down, underneath a white lab coat featuring a P Corp. employee card on the left lapel.

### Personality
Alessio acts very candidly, as seen by his brutal honesty during the briefing of the Fixers working on the eradication of La Manchaland. He cares about his work first and foremost, acting professionally as well.

### Story
Due to the damage that La Manchaland has done to the reputation of P Corp., as well as for bureaucratic reasons left unknown, the P Corp. Archival Department would begin hiring Fixers with the prospect of destroying the Bloodfiend amusement park the next time it emerged, with Alessio being involved in the development of the plan.
Alessio arrived to the command tent of those aiming to eradicate La Manchaland and its inhabitants, having been assigned the task of giving a briefing of the known information to the Fixers. He cooperated with Han-ul to inform everybody of the central areas located in La Manchaland, assigning groups to the three areas. As La Manchaland finally returned to the Backstreets of P Corp., he informed the group of their limited window of defeating the enemy.
