## Carmen

### Appearance
Carmen is a woman sporting long and choppy brown hair tied back into a ponytail and adorned with a red bear hair clip. She is seen wearing an olive-green, collared dress with four buttons, and a black miniskirt underneath. Over her dress, she dons a white lab coat draping past her knees. Her shoes are the color of her dress, with the toe to the heel being a darker shade similar to her skirt.

### Personality
Contradicting these accounts, however, are occasionally very concerning and intimidating depictions of Carmen. Netzach tells "X" that a voice came to him saying he must ask their "permission for anything. Even for death". Looks into Chesed's former life show Carmen claiming that she already knew without doubt that he would join her project, and that "no matter how great [he] may be, [he] will always be in the palm of [her] hand". The immense influence Carmen held over others sometimes carried an unshakably eerie undertone. Roland would form an iffy impression of her even through the Sephirah's praise.[1]
Carmen's true nature and personality ultimately come out to be as formless as her current state of self, with handfuls of different characters arguing in favor of vastly different impressions of her, and devastations caused with what she asserts are nothing but good intentions.

### Trivia
The red bear character on Carmen's hairclip is called "Kkomi" and can be seen worn by multiple background characters across certain *Limbus Company* CGs.
Carmen is the narrator of all Identity Stories that are not narrated by the Sinner themself.[2][3]
Carmen likes to claim that she's 170cm (5'7) tall.[4]
