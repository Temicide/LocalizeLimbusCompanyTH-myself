## Rain

### Appearance
Rain is a short man with black hair reaching his ears, with large orange-lens goggles resting over his bangs. He wears a black choker with a silver piece dangling at the center. He wears a large blue overcoat adorned with tools over his bare chest, with a similarly stocked toolbelt crooked over a pair of black swim trunks. He wears dirtied white sneakers on his feet. He is also shown to carry his weapon at times, a large sword with chainsaw-like teeth across its edge.

### Personality
Rain cares a lot about keeping up a good reputation for Molar Boatworks and will give unsubtle reminders to Olga to stay professional in front of their customers. While he might be strict, it is clear that it comes from a place of caring about Olga and Mika and a desire to keep working with them.

### Story
After the events of Library of Ruina* Rain and his fellow Molar Office Fixers, Olga and Mika, returned from their book forms and opened the Molar Boatworks workshop in the Backstreets of District 21. They are contracted by Limbus Company to modify Mephistopheles to be capable of nautical travel and while the Sinners spent days killing Trash Crabs to acquire the materials needed, Rain, Olga, and Mika complete the requested modifications. Despite some set backs with missing materials and forgetting to add the mode needed to keep Mephistopheles afloat, Rain and his friends soon saw LCB off on their journey across the Great Lake.
