## Tingtang Boss

### Appearance
The Tingtang Boss is a man with a cocky expression, wearing his brown hair in a slicked-back manner, the sides of his head shaved. He has two dot tattoos on his left temple, and a teardrop tattoo beneath his left eye, as well as having his right ear pierced with a two chain earrings hanging from it. He has a small moustache and beard, with a variety of lined tattoos along his entire body. He wears a green Hawaiian button-down shirt with a silver chain visible beneath it, over dark pants, with black shoes on his feet.
He is seen wielding a hooked shiv, which he utilizes in combat. The weapon has a pointed tip, and an open hole through its handle.

### Personality
The Tingtang Boss is not overly eager to start a fight, instead preferring to make others grovel and beg for forgiveness to avoid being killed. He hastily views others with disgust.

### Story
The Tingtang Gang was one of the four Syndicates competing in a card game for the Golden Bough that lies beneath the casino, with the Tingtang Boss being chosen to play in the game. The LCB met him when confronting the Tingtang Gang with the goal to take them down so they could infiltrate the casino posing as members, as well as to steal some of their Wishpower and clothing. The Syndicate and the Sinners engaged in a battle that leaves the gang members knocked unconscious. Later, when Rodion needed to infiltrate the poker game that the Tingtang Boss was meant to participate in, she poses as a representative of the Tingtang Gang.
