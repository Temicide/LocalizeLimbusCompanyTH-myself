## Dantes Notes

### Dante's Notes

**Dante's Notes** (*Hangul*: 단테의 노트) are a feature of the Theater first added to the game with the May 11th, 2023 update. It was originally described as "records and scribbles on various points of the City's setting as observed and written from [Dante's] perspective."[1]
*

### Overview

Dante's Notes are updated once a day at 6AM KST (with the daily reset), and, prior to Season 6, were known to be added throughout the two weeks leading up to a new Season. The first three releases of Dante's Notes received story content in conjunction with the first entry of the Season, but this trend was discontinued after Season 3. Additionally, Season 6 was initially skipped, with its entries eventually added alongside the LCB Regular Check-up BokGak during early Season 7. Afterwards, Season 7's notes were added with the release of new story content.
Thus far, Dante's Notes has run from:
May 11th, 2023 - May 31st, 2023 (Season 1)
October 19th, 2023 - November 1st, 2023 (Season 2)
March 14th, 2024 - March 28th, 2024 (Season 3)
September 26th, 2024 - October 10th, 2024 (Season 4)
April 30th, 2025 - May 15th, 2025 (Season 5)
March 19th, 2026 - April 2nd, 2026 (Season 7)
April 16th, 2026 - April 22nd, 2026 (Season 7)
