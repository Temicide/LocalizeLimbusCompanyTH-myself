**Tomah** (Hangul: 토마, *To-ma*) was a soldier of Old G Corp. who fought in The Smoke War, impressing a prominent memory upon Gregor that would later resurface. He is a supporting character in Canto I: The Outcast.

## Appearance
Tomah takes the form of a young man with blond hair and light-blue eye, with bandages on the right side of his face. He wears the Old G Corp. uniform, with his augmentations include a bug arm and two pairs of wings. As the Golden Bough continues to resonate with Gregor, he gradually grows greater insectile features, such as mandibles and pincers coming from his face.
## Personality
Tomah is a proud soldier that views Gregor as a hero, able to list off several facts about the man with ease and is comforted by the fact that Gregor is on the battlefield, trusting in him to help turn the tide in G Corp's favor. As the loops of Gregor's memories play out, Tomah's augmentations become more visible and disturbing, but he keeps his optimistic attitude and never displays a lack of faith in G Corp.
## Story
### Prior to Limbus Company*
Once working alongside Gregor and the rest of the Old G Corp., Tomah was a bright-eyed private who impressed a strong memory upon Gregor, even despite the dangerous setting of the Smoke War. While Tomah's true state was not elaborated upon, his absence outside of manifested memories implies he was a casualty of the war.
### Canto I: The Outcast
Tomah first appeared as a delusion formed during Gregor's synchronizing with the Golden Bough, grabbing onto Heathcliff's shirt to tell him and the other Sinners to escape due to the surrounding war. He praised Gregor's augmentations upon seeing them, noting that they were superior to everyone else's. When Gregor stated he never wanted to be a soldier, Tomah begins to mutate, telling Gregor to "take the lead". This scenario repeated twice until the last loop, where an already mutated Tomah pointed to the sky, stating he needs help and doesn't want to die, before being crushed by giant hands of Hermann.
