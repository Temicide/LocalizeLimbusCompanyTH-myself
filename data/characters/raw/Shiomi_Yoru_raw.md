Shiomi Yoru (Hangul: 시오미 요루), also known as the Dihui Star, is a member of the Pinky and one of the five parental figures assigned to training Yoshihide as a Nursefather of the House of Spiders. She is introduced in Canto IX: The Unsevering as the mother of Ryōshū.

## Appearance
Shiomi Yoru wears an ornate white kimono that conceals almost her entire body. The kimono is blue-accented, with several blue ribbons tied to her headwear, wrists, and obi. Her pale face is covered by a veil adorned with a flower pattern, which is also visible on the outfit's sleeves and hem. She wears white gloves and a long, uniquely-knotted rope that hangs from her neck to her waist. The Dihui Star's hair is black with blue streaks, falling past her shoulders. She carries an incredibly long ōdachi whose color scheme matches her robes.
Beneath her veil, her face is similar to Ryōshū's, though with longer hair with strands of blue strewn about.
## Personality
Little is known of Shiomi Yoru's personality prior to her joining of the House of Spiders, though she is shown to be a woman deeply mourning her loss of autonomy, which was taken away when the task of raising Ryōshū was forced onto her. Antithetical to her role as a member of the Pinky, she found herself bound to the House of Spiders, stuck without the freedom she desired.
Despite her own misery, Yoru was without sympathy for Ryōshū, since the child's birth all through her adulthood, and the Sinner often remembers Yoru belittling her or even threatening her through her pet Saru or Araya. Similar to the other Nursefathers, Shiomi Yoru carried a level of specific expectations for Ryōshū, most prominently during her time at the House of Spiders. Among the group, however, Shiomi Yoru treated her with a particular level of contempt, as Ryōshū seemed to her as a reflection of herself, and a constant reminder of the freedom and autonomy that had been forcefully stripped away from her.
She carried a bitter resentment of her protégé, choosing to openly admonish her for her connection to Araya, which reminded Yoru of her own connection to Ryōshū, as a daughter which she saw as a living fetter. Despite her burning disgust for Ryōshū and what she signified, she understood her own failings as a person and mentor, which she drilled into Ryōshū as something to avoid down to making a pinky promise with her, expressing bitterness and disappointment whenever Ryōshū failed to do so, and resembled her even in minor ways, such as smoking her same cigarettes and getting eye prosthetics that made her eyes resemble hers.
Through her pain and imprisonment in the House of Spiders, she understood her role as a stepping stone, showing grace—though tarnished with her hatred—even whilst being killed by the child she'd raised, egging her on to leave the House of Spiders and even seeing her own death as freedom. Even still, her distaste for the woman was all that remained following her death, haunting Ryōshū beyond Shiomi Yoru's time.
## Story
### Prior to Limbus Company
Shiomi Yoru was a member of the Pinky, the Dihui Star, famed for her genetic ability to perceive time perfectly, which made her a candidate to wield the Relic Arayashiki. By the will of the Five Fingers, Yoru was taken in the House of Spiders and made to birth a child, Ryōshū, then called Yoshihide.[1] Yoru was then made into a Nursefather of the House of Spiders, tasked with the upbringing and training of Yoshihide into the perfect wielder of Arayashiki. During her time at the House of Spiders, Yoru was figuratively and physically trapped, being forced to be a Nursefather, and being trapped in a series of maze-like Corridors that led exclusively to Rien's living space.[2] Years after Yoshihide, the Thumb would also bionengineer a child from Yoshihide's blood:[3] Araya, who would be taken in by Yoshihide as her own daughter.
Among the entire body of Nursefathers, Yoru was the closest in relation to Yoshihide; having been forced into the role of her mother, Yoru resented what Yoshihide represented, a fetter to her freedom and the cause of her aging body. Shortly after Yoshihide was brought to the House of Spiders, Yoru made a pinky promise with her that the girl would never grow to be like her.
The Dihui Star trained Yoshihide in a variety of fields, particularly swordplay. One particular lesson involved teaching Yoshihide of "the spiral", a weapon used to bore into a victim, though never completely, as a means to inflict massive distress and disfigurement. In her demonstration of this weapon, Yoru stabbed Yoshihide's pet dog, Saru, who was only saved by Yoshihide's speed in completing a mission. Though the pet survived, she was left forever struggling to breathe.
In their time together, Shiomi Yoru consistently reminded Yoshihide of her status as a fetter to the House of Spiders, her words at times so cruel that Yoshihide felt unable to speak up against her. One day, when Yoshihide returned home after receiving an augmentation that made her eye look identical to Yoru's, and upon seeing Yoshihide smoke the same cigarettes as her, Shiomi Yoru realized that, despite the promise they had made, Yoshihide was growing to resemble her more and more.
Eventually, around two years before the events of Canto IX, Yoshihide decided to free herself and Araya from the control of the House, leading to her attacking and wounding the various Nursefathers. As she was found by Yoshihide, Shiomi Yoru commented that this would be the only way for them to get even, before Yoshihide proceeded to sever her tongue. As she bled, Shiomi reminded her of the fact that neither of them would ever be free from the House of Spider's web, and that even Yoshihide would always remain stuck in her past, even if she forgot it. In a rage, Yoshihide stabbed the older woman in the chest, killing her then and there. The memory of Shiomi's death would later be lost to Ryōshū.
During the two years of Yoshihide's absence, Araya would emerge from the Time Vault, having aged dramatically thanks to the effects of temporal entanglement, to the point that she now resembled the deceased Shiomi Yoru. As a result of this resemblance, she was offered the position of the new Pinky Nursefather, which she accepted under the guise and mantle of the Dihui Star.
### Canto IX: The Unsevering
Due to Arayashiki's memory-erasing effects, Ryōshū's memory of killing Shiomi Yoru had been mutilated. During the House of Spiders' attack on Limbus Company HQ, Ryōshū comes face to face with the Dihui Star once more, believing her to be Shiomi Yoru somehow back from the dead; in reality, this was Araya disguised as Shiomi.
It is only when Limbus Company strikes back against the House during Operation Spider Pyre that Ryōshū remembers the truth. After defeating the Dihui Star in combat, Ryōshū stabs her through the heart, only to realize later that she had already killed Shiomi in a similar fashion; Shiomi is indeed long dead. It's only after Ryōshū strikes the Dihui Star down a second time that she realizes that she'd been fighting Araya all along.

## Trivia
Shiomi Yoru is likely based on the character Hu Sanniang (扈三娘，Pinyin: Hù Sānniáng) from Water Margin, who shares the title of Dihui Star.
In Water Margin, Hu Sanniang is the daughter of a squire and the 59th among the 108 Stars of Destiny, who is defeated in battle against the bandits of Liangshan, and captured by Song Jiang, one of their leaders. Song Jiang eventually convinces Hu Sanniang to join Liangshan, and arranges for her to marry the bandit leader Wang Ying, who ranks 58th among the 108 Stars.
