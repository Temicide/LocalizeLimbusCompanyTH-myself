**Pilot** (Hangul: 파일럿, *pa-il-leot*) is an Assistant Manager of the LCCB Preliminary Observation Unit 3, and a supporting character introduced in Canto V: The Evil Defining.

## Appearance
Pilot is a man with a youthful appearance, light brown skin and curly brown hair. He has deep brown eyes, with three circular marks in a horizontal pattern beneath the right one. He sports the LCCB uniform, consisting of a turquoise shirt and matching trousers, and a vest to which a communication device is attached. On his left earlobe, he has a small silver piercing.
## Personality
Pilot is an enthusiastic employee who takes pride in his job and holds deep admiration for his teammates as well as the members of the LCB. As the rookie of the team, he lacks the experience and knowledge that would be expected of an LCCB member and is naïve to many of the situations that he encounters on the job, sometimes making mistakes that brings great risk to the LCB. Even still, he values his work and tries his best to improve himself to be of greater use to the company.
## Story
### Canto V: The Evil Defining
As a member of the Before Team, Pilot joined the mission to travel to U Corp.'s Lobotomy Corp. Branch and survey it in search of the Golden Bough. Shortly finding the Bough, the team was attacked by the Twinhook Pirates, accompanied by members of the Middle, among which was the Big Brother Ricardo. Pilot's team leader, Haru, managed to convince the assailants that the team was actually led by Pilot, leading them to capture him, while the rest of the team remained trapped within the Branch as it was consumed by the Pallid Whale.
Pilot was then imprisoned in a storage container, and later freed by the LCB. Pilot delivered a message from Saude to the Sinners, revealing her to be still alive following the events of Canto III. At this point, he recounted of the team's encounter with the Twinhook Pirates and the Middle, and produced a hidden paper where he'd marked down the coordinates of the Lobotomy Corp. Branch. Pilot requested to join the Sinners on the mission, although Dante declined his offer as to not endanger him. During the interaction with the LCB, Pilot eventually started breaking down and mourning his team, and was comforted by the Sinners before they departed to complete their mission.
### Intervallo V: Nocturnal Sweeping
Some time prior to the Sinners' arrival at H Corp., Pilot would be tasked with finding a person connected to the Jia family in preparation for the LCB's future operations in the area. He was then approached by Qingtao, a former servant of the family. As the Sinners reached the District 8 Backstreets, Pilot, together with Saude, met up with them, and Pilot presented Qingtao to the Sinners as their guide through the Backstreets.
After the major events of the Intervallo, he appeared again with Saude to apologize for his mistake.
### Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing
At the start of the Canto, Pilot and Saude accompanied the Sinners and Jia Xichun's faction as they awaited the palanquins that would take them to Daguanyuan for H Corp.'s Family Hierarch evaluations. While Saude boarded the palanquin with the Sinners, Pilot stayed behind, informing Dante that he would have work to do outside of Daguanyuan.
### Canto IX: The Unsevering
Following the conclusion of Vergilius' separate travels, Pilot, who accompanied the man for an unknown portion of its time, was caught in the attack led by Valencina and her apprentice. While he remained in critical state, he managed to survive the attack. Following the conclusion of the House of Spiders' raid on Limbus Company HQ, he was among the injured given treatment via ampules.
