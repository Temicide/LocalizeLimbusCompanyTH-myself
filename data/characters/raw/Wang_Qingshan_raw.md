**Wang Qingshan** (Hangul: 왕청산, *wang-cheong-san*) is a member of the Wang family, as well as one of the many Family Hierarch Evaluation candidates competing for the role of Hierarch in District 8. She is a minor antagonist introduced in Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing.

## Appearance
Wang Qingshan wears her deep blue hair in a bowlcut fashion, with straight bangs around the entire perimeter of her head. She wears a brown kasaya, with an orange robe overtop fastened by a large golden buckle. On her feet, she wears plain brown sandals. When encountered during Canto VIII, she is bloodied and in a kneeling position, her clothes partially torn. She carries a japamala in her right hand.
## Personality
Desperately hoping to prove herself to her family, Wang Qingshan is a devout woman hoping to win the role of Hierarch, bringing honor to her family if she succeeds. Despite the fact that she fled from past battles, she is willing to battle while injured, hoping to simply prove herself as worth her weight.
## Story
### Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing
Wang Qingshan participated in the Family Hierarch Evaluations. She succeeded in the first round, and was met in the second round, being first identified by Lin Daiyu. Here, she encountered Jia Qiu who bested her in combat, barely escaping his brutal attack. Begging Hong Lu for his coins, the rejection pushed her to fear for her life if she returned home a failure, leading her to attack Hong Lu and the Sinners with her remaining troops. Losing the short scuffle, she was shortly eliminated.
### Intervallo VI: Spring Cultivation
Wang Qingshan was among the parties present during the hearing of Jia Xichun, held by Jia Yuanchun, who aimed to topple and belittle the newfound Hierarch.
Amidst the training of the new Hierarch, Wang Qingshan was one of the opponents of the Hierarch's training, serving as her third midterm. While she was greatly bothered by Jia Xichun, as well as annoyed by the use of her little brother in training, the two spar amicably. After the fact, while speaking to Jia Xichun, she is ultimately swayed to the side of the new Hierarch, now believing in the cause to make Hongyuan a safer place for the children.
During the end-of-training meeting, Wang Qingshan voted against the vote of no confidence held, allying herself with Jia Xichun.
