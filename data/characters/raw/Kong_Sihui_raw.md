**Kong Sihui** (Hangul: 공사혜, *gong-sahye*) was a member of the Kong Family, a scientist, and the loving wife of Kong Youjin. She was pulled into a scheme by Jia Mu that ultimately lead to the Kong Family's downfall. She is a supporting character in Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing.

## Appearance
Kong Sihui is an older woman with brown hair tied into a bun, fastened by a floral ornament and pin keeping it in place. She has two thick strands of hair reaching from her forehead to the tops of her ears. She wears a pink-and-gold hanfu with ornate golden patterning on the sleeves, and a floral pattern along the hemline of the skirt. She wears a translucent, pale blue cloth overtop her outfit.
In the period leading up to her death, Kong Sihui was pregnant.
## Personality
Kong Sihui was a very patient woman, interested in the sciences she participated in, assisting in resources for Jia Mu's experiments. She cared greatly for her husband and unborn child, hoping for a great future for the people of Hongyuan.
## Story
### Prior to the events of Limbus Company*
Kong Sihui once met with Jia Mu alongside Kong Youjin, who established a plan to create a thirteenth Heishou Pack Branch, invented by a Bolus created by the Hierarch accompanied by the team of Kong Family scientists. In reality, this was a front planned by Jia Mu to have the Kong Family annihilated through a city-wide Taboo that will result in the Head's direct intervention. The actual plan to annihilate the Kong family would come from a specialized Bolus Jia Mu gifted to Kong Sihui as supposedly a means to ensure good tidings for their unborn child. In actuality, the Bolus creates a Niaojia-ren, a type of Outskirts monster that reproduces by planting their young into other creatures. She consumed it and unbeknown to her, it was heavily implied that the Niaojia-ren consumed her child in her womb.
At the day of the presentation, Kong Sihui and Youjin would sit front row for the presentation, where the Niaojia-ren propagated by Jia Mu's special Bolus bursted out from her womb and killed her. The monster quickly multiplied, killing almost all of the attendees, and the Head arrived to clean up the rest alongside the monsters.
Their history would be twisted into a sign of greed among the royal houses of Hongyuan, painting a false history of hubris.
### Canto VIII: The Surrendered Witnessing
The history of the Kong Family would be made known to the Sinners and Lin Daiyu, as Hong Lu experiences memories reemerging via the resonance of a nearby Golden Bough.
