Vespa Crabro (Hangul: 베스파 크라브로, be-seu-pa keu-la-beu-lo) is a Color Fixer known as the Yellow Harpoon (Hangul: 노란작살, no-lan-jag-sal) and a member of the LCD, working alongside Moses and Ezra. He previously worked as a Taboo Hunter of N Corp., but was expelled from his position and began working with Moses' Office on jobs related to the Distortion Phenomenon.
Vespa was introduced in Chapter 15 of The Distortion Detective as an antagonist attempting to capture Taboo violators, but became a major supporting character following his expulsion from N Corp. In Limbus Company, he is a supporting character introduced in Canto IX: The Unsevering.

## Appearance
Vespa is a tall and muscular man with pale skin and short black hair with yellow highlights and blunt bangs that cover his forehead. He dons square glasses and has a tired look on his face. In Distortion Detective, he had his hair slicked back.
As a Taboo Hunter of N Corp., he wears a black skintight suit with a yellow symbol that resembles a wasp, black pants and shoes, and a yellow belt to which are attached two weapons―a hwando with a yellow blade and a harpoon created from the Relic Gungnir. He wears a yellow flight jacket with black highlights, which carries the same symbol as his shirt.
After his demotion, Vespa dons an ordinary black shirt and dress pants, and a gray-blue coat with a singular yellow streak running across its left side. He maintains the belt he uses to carry his weapons.
## Personality
Vespa is a serious and calculating individual, almost always acting in a manner that is both highly professional and detached from any emotions. He is well aware of his abilities and limits, frequently giving accurate assessments of how long a battle will take and never attempts to present himself as more capable than he actually is. Thanks to his time as a Taboo Hunter, he is highly observant and intelligent, able to quickly gather the basics of a situation and adjust his plans accordingly. This also makes him very adaptable, and he has a high level of mental fortitude that allows him to do even unpleasant things in order to survive, as seen by him turning to cannibalism to prevent starvation.
Despite this cold demeanor, Vespa has a sense of justice that he tries to follow and does not enjoy killing people needlessly. When talking to others for reasons unrelated to work, he respects peoples wishes when they ask him to not inquire about certain topics and rarely attempts to force his opinions on others. He has a fondness for vehicles and is a skilled driver, and enjoys cinnamon tea.
The only situation that gives Vespa trouble in following orders is when he's made to interact with Syndicates. Due to a tragic event in his past, Vespa prefers to kill Syndicate members rather than walk away and allow them to continue killing others. He has a particular hatred for the Thumb and is barely able to tolerate their presence. When Ezra discussed the Thumb's weapons and the various design elements of them that make them so well suited to taking lives, Vespa became upset to the point of drawing his sword on her in order to get her to stop discussing the Thumb's purges. Even with these struggles with Syndicates, Vespa still ultimately listens to Moses when she tells him to not cause them unnecessary trouble by instigating fights needlessly.
## Story
### Prior to The Distortion Detective
In the past, Vespa was seemingly a victim to one of the Thumb's purges but somehow managed to escape with his life.[2] The specifics of this event went unexplored in The Distortion Detective due to the series being cancelled, but he is still haunted by those memories and carries a deep grudge against the Thumb and Syndicates as a whole because of it. Presumably after his incident with the Thumb, Vespa found himself in the depths of the Ruins where he found the Relic "Gungnir"—a golden speartip.[3] Through a lucky coincidence, Vespa was able to wield this Relic and he fashioned it into a harpoon, with it acting as one of his primary weapons from then on.
Later in life, Vespa worked his way up to becoming a Grade 1 Fixer, getting numerous augmentations to strengthen his body, and eventually found employment as a Taboo Hunter of N Corp.
### The Distortion Detective
Vespa was assigned to hunt down YuRia after she violated one of N Corp.'s Taboos, which led him to Moses' Office. Moses covered for YuRia, who hid in plain sight as a teddy bear created by her Psychoment that her consciousness had recently been transferred into. Vespa was suspicious, but ultimately gave them his business card so they could contact him with any information on the Taboo violator and left. The women made plans to flee District 14 right away, but Vespa pursued them and quickly caught up with them and disposed of the Fixers they had hired to protect them throughout their escape. When Moses attempted to use her Psychoment to attack him, Vespa cut off her right arm to stop her and stored it in a preservation cube.
As Vespa's main goal was to destroy the footage that broke the Taboo, he opted to ask Moses about its whereabouts instead of immediately bringing her into N Corp. custody. Moses agreed to take him to the footage and asked YuRia to open her workshop door, but Vespa refused to enter first given how untrustworthy the location was. His suspicions were proven correct as YuRia immediately began to attack him with countless teddy bears that emerged from her workshop, although he fended them off with ease. Ezra then arrived to protect Moses, and her and Vespa had an intense battle that ultimately ended with Vespa being tricked into entering the workshop and being locked inside.
Vespa was trapped inside the workshop with no method of escape for two weeks, and eventually was forced to eat Moses' arm that he had with him in order to survive. Fortunately for Vespa, Moses and her assistants had found themselves in a dangerous situation while in Quercus Village and had to open the door and free Vespa in order to survive. While upset with Moses and her companions for breaking his trust, he recognized the danger of the dozens of Distortions that were attacking all of them and fought alongside them. After the Distortions were subdued, Han Hee-joon arrived to discuss matters with Moses and handed off a letter from N Corp. to Vespa that informed him he had been expelled from his position as a Taboo Hunter due to his failure with this job.
Han Hee-joon took Vespa, Moses, Ezra, and YuRia to one of Dias' mansions to discuss hiring them to retrieve Distortions from within Nest L. Despite their recent conflicts, Vespa agreed to work with Moses' Office for Dias after being promised a chance to be reinstated with N Corp. and the group set off the next day. Once within District 12, they quickly captured the Distortions 'The Laundry of Dreams' and 'The Human Thunderbolt' and contained them within their van's cargo compartment which was created as a dimensional container. The latter's powers caused the van to break down, however, and the group realized they would be unable to make it to safety before the Night in the Backstreets began. After surviving two waves of Sweepers, they were granted shelter by some members of the Thumb that had occupied a home before the Night came.
Due to his past with the Thumb, Vespa was incredibly reluctant to cooperate with them, but respected Moses' orders to not cause trouble. He transferred the authority he had as a Grade 1 Fixer according to the Thumb's hierarchy system to Moses and allowed her to negotiate a deal where they would track down the Thumb's missing ammunition delivery and the Thumb would provide her with information on Distortions in the area. This led them to discovering that the Distortion 'The Marksman of the Mist' had killed several members of the Zwei Association who had just defeated the Syndicate that created the Thumb's bullets. A higher grade Fixer of the Zwei appeared to get revenge for his fallen comrades, and Moses had the team switching allegiances between the Fixer, the Thumb, and the Distortion in order to survive. Ultimately, they sided with the Zwei Fixer and Vespa was able to kill the Thumb members in order to prove their sense of justice to him.
Afterwards, the group traveled to the Nest of the fallen Wing and decided to check into a hotel for a proper night of sleep. This hotel was revealed to be run by the Bloodfiend Elder of L Corp., although what happened during their stay there is unknown as The Distortion Detective was cancelled in the middle of this arc.
### Limbus Company
Vespa ultimately followed Moses and Ezra to work for Limbus Company's Distortion Department.
Canto VII: The Dream Ending
Vespa was mentioned to be working off screen while Moses and Ezra video called the LCB.
Canto IX: The Unsevering
When the Five Fingers raided Limbus Company HQ, Vespa was in the middle of completing his registration to become a Color Fixer with the Hana Association. Once the forms were completed, Vespa rushed to the HQ to protect his colleagues. He spotted Dante's group first and assisted them in their fight against Sora, before retreating to meet up with Moses' group once the fight was over. After meeting up with the former's group, he fought against Matthias to protect the wounded LCD members, before Matthias called off the fight after receiving an order to retreat.
Later, the surviving members of Limbus Company decided to launch a counter-raid on the House of Spiders in order to retrieve the Golden Boughs that had been stolen from them. Moses and the members of the LCD planned to participate in the counter-raid, excluding Aeng-du who was still recovering from her injuries during the raid.
The LCD, alongside Don Quixote, Hong Lu and Yi Sang entered the Middle's Corridor within the House. The group engaged in battle with Matthias and Kira. Alongside Ezra, Vespa clashed with the Nursefather. Nearing the end of the battle, Kira attacks Vespa and locks him in place, giving Matthias the opportunity to finish off Vespa. To her and Vespa's surprise, Matthias slashes at both of them. While Vespa survived Matthia's attack, Kira was mortally injured. Shortly after, Moses bores a hole through Matthias's torso using her Crimson Point, and Vespa finishes him off by slicing through his head. The battle ends with Matthias dead, and with Kira apprehended.

## Trivia
Vespa and Crabro are both genera of wasp. Vespa is the genus of hornets, and Crabro is a genus of square-headed wasps.
Additionally, "Vespa crabro" is the binomial name of European hornets.
Though it could be a coincidence as yellow and black are colors often associated with bees and wasps, Vespa donning a yellow jacket during his time as an N Corp. Taboo Hunter can be a reference to the Yellow jacket, another species of wasp.
Vespa's Relic takes its name from Gungnir, the spear Odin uses in Norse mythology which has the ability to always hit its target.
According to Moses, Vespa's Distortion manifests as a pair of tattered wings of a bee.[4]
Vespa's hobbies are vehicle maintenance and forming his thoughts while brewing cinnamon tea.
This matches two of his likes, being cars and cinnamon; he also likes foodstuffs aswell as clothes well suited for agile activities.
Vespa dislikes cacti and Syndicates, especially the Thumb.
Ezra came up with the title of Yellow Harpoon as an option for Vespa's Color title, which ultimately was the one chosen by the Hana Association.
