**Alfonso** (Hangul: 알폰소, *al-pon-so*) is the director of K Corp., an enigmatic woman focused on running her corporation at any means necessary. She is a minor antagonist in Canto IV: The Unchanging.

## Appearance
Alfonso is a tall, average built woman with pale white skin and long black hair with neon green highlights, extending down to her knees. In contrast to her wavy hairstyle, Alfonso's bangs are cut flat, resting just at her brow line. She has a mysterious barcode tattoo or marking below her left eye, with seams from the bottom of her eyes to her jaw. In illustrated cutscenes, Alfonso has bright green eyes. Alfonso's attire consists of a black collared shirt, a dull greenish-gray blazer, necktie, long suit pants and gloves of similar color and black dress shoes and socks. The blazer, suit pants and tie all have a geometrical, diamond design cut into them with bright green accents, primarily on her left hip and shoulders.
## Personality
Alfonso is a generally forward and blunt woman, primarily caring about the benefit and profits that will be brought to her Wing, as shown by her directly questioning Dongrang's use in the past during the Golden Bough's resonance. She shows little care for those that she and the other researchers of K Corp. torture so that they may draw out more tears from the Singularity the group uses, seeing it as a necessary point of bloodthirst for the Wing to remain operating. She shows herself to be further emotionally distant as she shows little care of Dongrang's death, even further using it to save face for the Wing.
## Story
### Prior to Limbus Company*
At some point in the past, following Stephanette's death, a council of high-ranking K Corp. employees gathered to discuss who would become the new director for the happenings of the Wing. With a dispute emerging, some of the employees would begin to attack one another, to which Alfonso would gain the idea of using violence to harvest tears from the Tearful Thing.
Over the course of many meetings, Alfonso discussed the torture of others with Dongrang, as well as urging him to participate at times to better produce tears via the Singularity.
### Canto IV: The Unchanging
Alfonso does not first physically appear, though contacted Dongrang over the phone early on in Canto IV, discussing matters between him and Samjo, and, as mentioned by her later, saying goodbye to the researcher as she knew the oncoming fate of the two.
Alfonso was later seen during the Golden Bough's resonance showing past moments of Dongrang and her history, with her discussing the benefits that working with Dongrang will bring K Corp., whilst the two admire the Tearful Thing. Even further from this point, the two would be seen harvesting tears from the Singularity through the torture of one of K Corp.'s employees, discussing the inevitable use of footage harvested from Wings and Syndicates so that tears can be saved from being used during the torture of others.
Alfonso finally appeared physically at the end of Canto IV, meeting with the Sinners following the death of Dongrang and the retrieval of the K Corp.'s Golden Bough. She took the time to explain how they will cover up the events, with Dongrang taking the entirety of the blame, painting him as a deranged employee. She adds criticism of Dante's leadership, much to their chagrin, though Faust intercepted their discussion. Alfonso denied the possibility of the LCB making a contract to not speak of the Wing's Singularity, and instead has their memories of the event wiped, leaving them only with the knowledge that they saw something, but not knowing what they happened upon during their time in the Wing, something that affected all of them besides Dante.
